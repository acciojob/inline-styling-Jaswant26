Flowers photos

Flowers are the reproductive part of a plant. They produce seeds, which eventually become other flowers. Flowers are made up of a floral axis, which contains the essential reproductive organs, and accessory organs, such as sepals and petals. The accessory organs protect the essential organs and attract pollinating insects.

Roses pictures

Roses are a type of shrub that are known for their beauty. They are a member of the Rosaceae family and have the scientific name Rosa. Roses come in many colors, including red, blue, yellow, orange, pink, purple, white, and black. They are often associated with love, romance, and sometimes secrecy.
